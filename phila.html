    We are very excited to be hosting this year’s Reunion Weekend festivities at the Sheraton Valley Forge Hotel in King of Prussia. This location is easily accessible from both the Schuylkill Expressway and the Pennsylvania Turnpike. Explore historic attractions, hike or bike in Valley Forge National Historical Park, try your luck at Valley Forge Casino Resort, or do some shopping at King of Prussia Mall, the East Coast’s largest retail and shopping mall – all within a few miles of the hotel. Be sure to check out what’s new in Center City Philadelphia which is only a short drive away.

    Come early to explore the city! This is a welcoming place with new and one-of-a-kind attractions, an abundance of art and culture, thrilling entertainment options and unforgettable shopping!
